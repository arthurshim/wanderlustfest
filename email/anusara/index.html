The second annual Wanderlust Festival returns to the stunning peaks of Squaw Valley USA in Lake Tahoe, CA from July 29 to August 1, 2010. Wanderlust was grateful and honored to have John Friend and so much of the Anusara Kula participating in its inaugural event. In 2010, Anusara and Wanderlust areworking closely together to present an integrative, educational and wildly thrilling communal space. Come join The Merry Band and artistically offer your individual light and your own unique music to the heartfelt prayer of adding more beauty, love, and goodness to the world. Drink from the exquisite and intoxicating nectar of Tantric Yoga Philosophy while Hoop Dancing with some of the greatest hoopers in the country. Don't be surprised as you wander through the Village between practices finding yourself learning to balance on a slackline with the YogaSlacker's.


Or exploring all the freedoms of expression and connection that come with reclaiming the joy of the child within at the "Play Lounge." Learning to Fly (or be flown) with Jason & Jenny co-founder's of AcroYoga and their tribe. All the while deepening your Asana practice with master teachers Elena Brower, Sianna Sherman, and much more...

Be sure to grab your tickets before the advance prices expire! For a limited time, you can pick up a three-day Seeker pass for $340 and a four-day Sage pass for $420.
